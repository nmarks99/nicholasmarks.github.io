Scratch Built Carbon Fiber High Power Rocket

For the 2020-2021 NASA Student Launch Competition, as Launch Vehicle lead on the NUSTARS (Northwestern University Space Technology & Rocketry Society) rocket team, I led the design and construction of the launch vehicle which utilized various carbon fiber manufacturing techniques such as filament winding, vacuum bagging, and hand-layups.

The top photo shows the completed booster section made from 100% in-house manufactured cargon fiber. In the bottom image you can see the CAD model of the booster which was used to aid in the design and manufacturing processes.

Photo of the NUSTARS team (Me: center) with the rocket on the launch pad at a test launch on 3/20/2021
